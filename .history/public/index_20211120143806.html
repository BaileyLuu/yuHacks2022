About yuHacks

yuHacks is York University’s 24-hour virtual hackathon by students, for students. We spent months interviewing fellow students to understand how we could best serve them through our hackathon. The predominant theme was that students wanted a focus on as they prepare to enter a highly competitive modern workforce. Through yuHacks, we hope to present attendees with the opportunity to better their professional selves while engaging in all the fun you’d expect from a hackathon.

Sustainability

Have an idea for a hack that looks to solve pressing environmental issues? This challenge is open to teams who want to tackle big problems like climate change, food waste, and biodiversity loss (just to name a few).

New Hacker

New to hackathons? Don't know how to code? No worries! You will be provided with resources to learn and be successful at yuHacks. This challenge is opened to teams where majority of the members are attending their first hackathon.

Non-technical

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

  • A hackathon is a design sprint-like event; often, in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, domain experts, and others collaborate intensively on software projects. At yuHacks, we have made it possible for those without technical skills to still get in on the fun! Check out our tracks for more information!

I applied, when will decisions be released?

  • All application decisions will be made on a rolling basis! The sooner you apply, the sooner you’ll hear back!

Who can participate?

  • Any student from any institution is welcome to attend! Whether you’re in high school, college, university, or even a coding bootcamp, you are more than welcome to join in on the fun!

What if I’ve never been to a hackathon before?

  • If you’ve never done a hackathon before, you’re definitely in the right place to change that! We have put together a track just for you!

What kind of events will there be?

  • Our specific event focus will be professional development. You can expect several talks from industry experts, recruiters, and other incredibly talented people! You can also expect several workshops and games to help you enjoy your time away from hacking.

Where will yuHacks take place?

  • yuHacks will be hybrid. It means that the hackathon will take place in-person and online at the same time!

How much does it cost to attend yuHacks?

  • There will be no cost involved with attending yuHacks.

How many people can be on a team?

  • Teams can be anywhere from 1-4 hackers.

When do applications open?

  • Applications will open on January 1st, 2022!

What platforms will be used on event day?

  • Leading up to event week, you’ll receive all important information via email. Our Discord server will open up at the start of event week. Event day communications will all take place in the Discord server. Most of our events will be broadcast live on YouTube.

I’m interested in sponsoring your event!

  • We’d love to talk about how we can work together! Please send an email to yuhacks2022@gmail.com and we’ll be sure to get back to you ASAP.
