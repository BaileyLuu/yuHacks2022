About yuHacks

yuHacks is York University’s 24-hour virtual hackathon by students, for students. We spent months interviewing fellow students to understand how we could best serve them through our hackathon. The predominant theme was that students wanted a focus on as they prepare to enter a highly competitive modern workforce. Through yuHacks, we hope to present attendees with the opportunity to better their professional selves while engaging in all the fun you’d expect from a hackathon.
